Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients, baked at a high temperature traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

The two most popular types of pizza are the traditional Neapolitan pizza from Naples and the Roman pizza from Rome. However, many other regional and international variations exist.

Originating from Naples, Italy, pizza has become one of the most popular foods in the world, with a wide variety of toppings and styles.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Various toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to its highest setting.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add desired toppings.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Garnish with fresh basil if using, and serve hot.
